The product

A monthly physical mail experience: letters, postcards, printed fragments, city prompts, and small collectible objects.

每月寄出的實體信件體驗：文字、明信片、紙本碎片、城市任務與可收藏小物。

The discovery

Before the club opens, selected letters are placed around Hong Kong. If you find one, enter the password to reveal where it belongs — not the full story.

正式開放前，我們會在香港不同角落放置信件。找到的人，可以用信封上的密碼查看它來自哪個地點。
